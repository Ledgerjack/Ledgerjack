# Accountant pack — rung 1 (make it clean and standard)

Goal: make what a client sends their accountant so standard that accountants are
glad to receive it — the low-cost, no-backend step toward accountant adoption.

## What changed (Settings -> Your accountant)
- "Download accountant pack" button. For the current tax year (derived from the
  region fiscal-year start; verified UK 6 Apr to 5 Apr, correct across boundary):
  provisional statements + SA103 boxes (one CSV) and the full transactions CSV.
- The shared note now leads with a provisional SA103 headline: turnover, total
  expenses, net profit. Everything is clearly labelled PROVISIONAL.

## Why it matters
An accountant receiving figures already mapped to the SA103 boxes can work with
them with no re-keying — building goodwill and word-of-mouth.

## Rung 2 (later, needs a backend)
A live, read-only, multi-client accountant portal turns "willing to receive it"
into "recommends it to clients". Signposted in-app as coming soon.
